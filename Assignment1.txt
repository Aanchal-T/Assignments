4) TAG: Tags are used for writing an effective HTML code. HTML contains tags for each function.Tags are made of opening and closing diamond brackets such as: <HTML>,<BODY>. Mostly tags are used with opening and closing tag.
Closing tags are a bit different from opening tags. 
Opening tags are made up of opening and closing diamond brackets with the tag name in between. Whereas Closing tags are made up by opening diamond bracket followed by / and the tag name and later on by a closing diamond bracket.

ELEMENT: An element is made up of an opening and a closing tag. such as: <body></body> will be known as body element.
